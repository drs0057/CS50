I have been learning Spanish for about 3 months now through the language learning app Babble. Babble is a fun and interactive app that really facilitates actually learning and retaining information. I took two years of Spanish in high school and figured it was time to double down on that investment and achieve a semi-fluent level of Spanish.

Let's have a quick Spanish lesson!

Most Spanish verbs ending in -er replace the ending with -o when you are referrring to yourself.
Conjucate the following verbs:

Yo la comida.
Translation: I eat the food.

Te !
Translation: I see you!

Yo el libro.
Translation: I read the book.
